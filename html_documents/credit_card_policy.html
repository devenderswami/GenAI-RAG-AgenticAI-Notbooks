Credit Card Billing and Payment Policy

Customers must ensure timely payment of their credit card bills to avoid late payment fees and interest charges.

The due date for payment will be mentioned on every monthly statement.

Partial payments will attract interest on the remaining balance until full payment is made.

Rewards and Cashback

Reward points can be redeemed for vouchers, merchandise, or converted into statement credit.
